Daffodil
The daffodil symbolises rebirth and new beginnings. It's one of the first flowers to bloom at the end of winter, announcing the beginning of spring

jasmine
The sweetly-scented jasmine flower can fill a room or a garden with its heady scent. It often symbolizes love, beauty or passion. Its pure white blossoms can also symbolize purity.
Tulip
Tulips are the quintessential spring blooming bulb. The wide range of colors, sizes, and patterns make them a spring favorite. The most known meaning of tulips is perfect and deep love.
